About

Hi! My name is Kaylyn Kuehn. I grew up near Bozeman, MT. Since I can remember, I've always loved painting and drawing. When I was in high school, I found a way to apply creativity into the business world with graphic design. I took as many classes as I could and even had the opportunity of visiting Dallas, TX and Anaheim, CA during the Business Professionals of America Nationals competitions. After graduating high school, I moved to Coeur d'Alene to study Graphic and Web Design at North Idaho College.
